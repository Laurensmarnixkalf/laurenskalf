About

My name is Laurens,

I like to learn and become better at what I enjoy doing. I try to spend most of my days doing exactly that :) *And drinking, preferrably good, coffee while at it* My personality is probably for the most part characterized by my determination. I have a strong intrinsic motivation to make the most out of everything I take on. I am an energetic, creative analytical thinker. I'm heavily into sports and my work: both I like to do in teams. In my experience, the group dynamics and the surplus of the team as a whole determine its success. Often people around me tell me I can be contentious, and always look for ways to improve myself and the people around me. I guess for me it is an art to keep a balance in everything I do.
